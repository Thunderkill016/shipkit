# Discover opportunity

Use when the human asks what to build, requests an innovative or breakthrough
feature, presents an uncertain idea, or needs current market or technology
research.

## Read first

- `IDEA.md`
- current project model when available
- `docs/ai/DISCOVERY_RESEARCH.md`
- `docs/ai/SAFETY.md`

## Required sequence

1. Frame one decision and outcome.
2. Inspect internal evidence.
3. Plan research streams and stop conditions.
4. Prefer direct and primary sources; record dates and source limitations.
5. Maintain supporting and contradicting evidence.
6. Map opportunities before generating solutions.
7. Generate multiple mechanisms, not cosmetic variations.
8. Rank important uncertain assumptions.
9. Design the cheapest decision-changing experiment.
10. Record an explicit decision and retrospective.

Do not edit product code during discovery and never invent user evidence.

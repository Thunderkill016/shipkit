# Validate or kill an existing idea

Read `AGENTS.md` and `docs/ai/DISCOVERY_RESEARCH.md`.

Idea:

[Describe the proposed idea.]

Do not advocate for the idea and do not implement it.

- Restate target user, triggering context, expected outcome, and mechanism.
- List desirability, usability, feasibility, viability, and integrity
  assumptions.
- Rank assumptions by importance and uncertainty.
- Inspect internal evidence and current primary external sources.
- Find substitutes, incumbent responses, failed approaches, complaints, and
  reasons users may not switch.
- Record supporting and contradicting evidence with dates and citations.
- Compare at least three materially different mechanisms, including a
  non-feature alternative.
- Define success and kill criteria before choosing an experiment.
- Recommend the cheapest experiment that can falsify the riskiest assumption.
- End with reject, park, research, prototype, technical spike, or delivery.
- State exactly what evidence would reverse the recommendation.

# Discover a valuable opportunity

Read `AGENTS.md`, `IDEA.md`, `docs/ai/DISCOVERY_RESEARCH.md`, and the current
project model.

Decision or desired outcome:

[Describe the decision, target user, and outcome.]

Work in discovery mode. Do not modify product code.

1. Create a dated folder under `docs/ai/research/` from the templates.
2. Inspect repository and internal evidence before public web research.
3. Write explicit unknowns, queries, preferred sources, decision-changing
   evidence, timebox, and stop conditions.
4. Prefer direct and primary sources. Record URL, publication date, and access
   date for material claims.
5. Maintain an evidence ledger and distinguish fact, user statement, source
   interpretation, and agent inference.
6. Search deliberately for contradiction, failed approaches, substitutes, and
   reasons not to build.
7. Map opportunities before solutions.
8. Generate at least twelve concepts across at least four mechanisms, including
   a non-feature and conservative option.
9. Red-team the leading concepts after evidence is visible.
10. Identify the most important uncertain assumption and the cheapest experiment
    capable of changing the decision.
11. Finish with reject, park, research, prototype, technical spike, or delivery.
12. Complete the retrospective and update the research index.

Never describe novelty as value without an outcome and mechanism.

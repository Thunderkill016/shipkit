## Principles adopted

- keep tasks small and issue-shaped;
- plan before non-trivial implementation;
- keep durable context in the repository;
- use a short instruction map rather than a giant manual;
- give agents executable pass/fail signals;
- separate implementation and review context;
- increase control when consequences increase;
- convert repeated mistakes into automation.
